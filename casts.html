CAMILO MIRANDA


Camilo is truly one of the most captivating characters of the balearic scene in Ibiza. His extensive knowledge of electronic music (and beyond) has earned him a remarkable reputation not only in Ibiza but throughout Spain and Europe.As a resident of the esteemed "Pikes" and co-owner, alongside Luca Averna and Christian Len, of the renowned label/collective "HOMIES," Camilo has been instrumental in curating unforgettable experiences since 2015. Their unique balearic concept invites DJs to share their rare and cherished musical gems.

ADRIAN MARTH


No need to say how much we like the vibe of Italo Moderni and Adrian Marth. The barcelona's based capo, big fan of synths, EBM and Newbeat; has been building recently a solid spot on the scene, being a big influence for dj's who focus their sound on the Italo era.In this new cast, Adrian Marth dig on his huge vinyl collection delivering a very special mix where (of course) "Italo-Disco" will be the leitmotiv of the story. STATUES


We started following Statues since we discovered this little gem that is "River Darkness" included in the mighty compilation done by "Claremont 56" for his Ten Anniversary. From that moment on we've always had an ear under every release or mix that comes from Statues Collective. Statues has gained a well deserved place on the scene delivering excellent works that have find a place on Eclectics. Is it Balearic? and the mentioned Claremont 56; and being remixed by some MM Discos favourites like Coyote, Gold Suite or The Vendtetta Suit among many others.
