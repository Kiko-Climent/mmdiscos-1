MOGWAA

Hazzy Dreams


Tracklist:

  • a1 - Levitation
  • a2 - Nacimiento
  • b1 - Moondance
  • b2 - Soothing

Review:

Pairing a sensitivity to the construction of ambient, funk, bossa and cassette-tape 80s experiments with his own cinematic subtlety,Hazy Dreams takes a gentle, minimalistic approach, crafting its own escapist world that offers a welcome diversion from the steady flow of busy balearica and downtempo. An exercise in simplicity, and more proof of the ever-expanding musical horizons of one of the scene's most virtuosic instrumentalists nowadays.

VARIOUS ARTISTS

MM Discos Factory Edits


Tracklist:

  • a1 - JAISIEL 'Timeless'
  • a2 - TREPANADO 'Abrir Fogo'
  • b1 - TECH SUPPORT 'Space Dream'
  • b2 - CLAP KENT 'El Jaguar'

Review:

Our aim was to honor the dance music from the enchanting early 90s, especially the vibrant and influential scene that thrived in Valencia, inspiring countless DJs worldwide. Taking the reins, we have Jaisiel, Tech Support, Trepanado, and Clap Kent, who are presenting 4 fantastic edits that will transport us directly back to the golden age of electronic music in Spain, known as "La Ruta del Bakalao." Prepare to dance and let the waves of nostalgia engulf you!

FLORIN BÜCHEL

The Lost Tapes


Tracklist:

  • a1 - The Lost Body
  • a2 - The Lost Mind
  • b1 - The Lost Soul


Review:

Florin Büchel has joined MM Discos, and to celebrate this occasion, we've unearthed some old material that had been tucked away for many years. These are three tracks produced in 2010, featuring Zurich's best-kept secret taking you on a synthetic intergalactic voyage. In this musical journey, vocoders, analog synthesizers, and drum machines come together to create a time capsule, transporting you straight into the subculture of the 1980s.

LONGHAIR

Axis Mundi EP.


Tracklist:

  • a1 - Sistema Solar
  • a2 - Night Rider
  • b1 - One More Flight
  • b2 - Moment Of Peace

Review:

With the 'Axis Mundi EP,' Longhair showcases their impressive versatility by seamlessly merging various subgenres, spanning from indie dance and leftfield house to Nu-Disco and everything in between. They infuse their unique cosmic touch and incorporate some trance elements, resulting in a record that's equally suitable for the dance floor while sporting our favorite dancing shoes or for relaxed home listening on our sofa. An outragenous musical journey.

BONNIE & KLEIN

Roof Garden EP.


Tracklist:

  • a1 - Accasun
  • a2 - Notes From The Road
  • b1 - Diamante
  • b2 - Albatros

Review:

Hailing from the Mediterranean Sea, the Greek duo Bonnie & Klein have made their debut on MM Discos with the release of "ROOF GARDEN," an EP where balearic beats and downtempo music take center stage. These two devotees of Balearic vibes have long been inspired by Greece's architecture and culture, and they extend an invitation to embark on an incredible journey from the Ionian coast, through the Aegean beaches, all the way to the Myrtle Sea.
