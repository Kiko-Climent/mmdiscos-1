DISCOS SINCE 2015

MOGWAA

Hazzy Dreams


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - Levitation
  • a2 - Nacimiento
  • b1 - Moondance
  • b2 - Soothing

Review:

Pairing a sensitivity to the construction of ambient, funk, bossa and cassette-tape 80s experiments with his own cinematic subtlety,Hazy Dreams takes a gentle, minimalistic approach, crafting its own escapist world that offers a welcome diversion from the steady flow of busy balearica and downtempo. An exercise in simplicity, and more proof of the ever-expanding musical horizons of one of the scene's most virtuosic instrumentalists nowadays.


VARIOUS ARTISTS

MM Discos Factory Edits


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - JAISIEL 'Timeless'
  • a2 - TREPANADO 'Abrir Fogo'
  • b1 - TECH SUPPORT 'Space Dream'
  • b2 - CLAP KENT 'El Jaguar'

Review:

We wanted to pay homage to the dance music of those magical early 90s, particularly that bustling and influential scene that happened in Valencia and that inspired many DJs all around the world. We've got Jaisiel, Tech Support, Trepanado, and Clap Kent taking charge, delivering this time 4 amazing edits that will transport us right back to that golden era of electronic music in Spain "La Ruta del Bakalao". Get ready to dance and let the nostalgia wash over you!


FLORIN BÜCHEL

The Lost Tapes


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - The Lost Body
  • a2 - The Lost Mind
  • b1 - The Lost Soul


Review:

Florin Büchel lands in MM Discos and for the occasion we've rescued this old material that has been stored for many years, 3 tracks produced back in 2010 where Zurich's best kept secret propose a synthetic-intergalactic-journey, where vocoders, analog synths and drum boxes play around in order to build a time capsule that travels straight to the 80's subculture. Captain Büchel at the controls of the spacecraft on our endless journey towards new horizons.

STATUES

Lost Star EP.


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - Conney Island
  • a2 - Lost Star
  • b1 - The becomming
  • b2 - Club Love

Review:

The Sun, that magical star that provides energy and vitality, aims to hide from us and disappear during the long, dark winter times. Perhaps all things in the universe need a break and maybe that's why it is lost during this period. But just a little frame of time, cause he always come back to shine more than ever. Fronted by Bradley Lucke and Mark Crooks, UK collective Statues has definitely established in the MM Discos family signing the last release by doing a very special tribute to the sun



LONGHAIR

Axis Mundi EP.


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - Sistema Solar
  • a2 - Night Rider
  • b1 - One More Flight
  • b2 - Moment Of Peace

Review:

Longhair shows with 'Axis Mundi EP' their great versatility blending different subgenres that vary from Indie dance, leftfield house, Nu-Disco and everything in between and adding their particular cosmic touch and some trancy elements. A record that will work great either at the club wearing our favourite dancing shoes or chilling at home at our sofa. a space oddity that rises from our living room all the way to the solar system, Earth, Saturn and Pluto until the end of the Galaxy.



BONNIE & KLEIN

Roof Garden EP.


    Tracklist:

  • a1 - Accasun
  • a2 - Notes From The Road
  • b1 - Diamante
  • b2 - Albatros

Review:

Straight from the Mediterranean Sea, the Greek Duo Bonnie and Klein have landed in MM Discos to present “ROOF GARDEN”, an EP where the balearic beats and downtempo takes over the control and where the “2 Balearica devotes” invite us to enjoy an amazing trip from the Ionian coast thru the Aegean beaches until Myrtle Sea. Bonnie & Klein are no longer unknown after being working the last 4 years for well renown labels as “Leng”, “Music for Dreams” and “Is It Balearic?” among others.
